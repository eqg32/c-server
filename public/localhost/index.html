Mountain Landscape

This is a simple webpage displaying a peaceful mountain scene. The image below shows a serene natural landscape with mountains, trees, and a calm lake.

Mountains represent stability and endurance in nature. They are some of the most ancient geological formations on our planet.

Interesting Facts

  • Mountains cover approximately 24% of Earth's land surface.
  • The highest mountain on Earth is Mount Everest at 8,848 meters.
  • Mountains are home to about 15% of the world's population.
  • Many rivers have their sources in mountain regions.
